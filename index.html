EMS TRAINING

EMS (Electrical Muscle Stimulation) fitness technology uses low-frequency electrical impulses to stimulate muscles, enhancing workout efficiency. Originally used in physical therapy, EMS is now popular in fitness due to its ability to target muscle groups more intensively in a shorter time, helping improve strength, tone, and endurance.
